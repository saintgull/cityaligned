City Aligned

Weekly gatherings for interesting people in cities around the world. Come sit down, introduce yourself, and meet people.

What is this?

Every city has interesting people who should bump into each other more often. City Aligned is a network of standing, weekly meetups that make that happen.

No agenda. No sign-up. No presentations. Just a fixed time and place where you know you'll find good conversation. Show up once or every week. The table is always there.

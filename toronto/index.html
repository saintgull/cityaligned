Toronto

Every Friday at 7:30 PM

What?

Every Friday at Victory Cafe (Bloor & Bathurst), 7:30 PM until about 10, or until you've made other plans. Look for the table with the horse figurine, sit down, and say hi.

When
Fridays, 7:30 PM
Where
Victory Cafe, Bloor & Bathurst
Find us
Look for the horse figurine

Who?

Smart, curious people who like talking about AI safety, rationality, and how the world works, plus anyone they've had a good conversation with. It started with a few people we'd met in Toronto, and grew from there. No application, no vetting. Just show up.

Why?

Because Toronto is full of these people, and they should bump into each other more often. The table has run every week for about a year now, usually fifteen or so people and a lot of them regulars by this point, to where it's less a meetup than a group of people who actually know each other.

Toronto

Every Friday at 7:30 PM

What?

A weekly public gathering every Friday at Victory Cafe (Bloor & Bathurst) starting at 7:30 PM and going until roughly 10, or until you make other plans. Look for the table with the horse figurine, come sit down, introduce yourself, and meet people.

When
Fridays, 7:30 PM
Where
Victory Cafe, Bloor & Bathurst
Find us
Look for the horse figurine

Who?

It starts with people we've had good conversations with in Toronto, and then anyone they've had a good conversation with, too. The idea is simple: an open, standing invite to come meet interesting people at a specific place and time. No application, no vetting. Just show up.

Why?

Because Toronto is full of interesting people who should bump into each other more often. A standing, low-key weekly slot makes it simple to keep showing up and slowly turn strangers into a real community.

Warsaw

A new table

What?

Warsaw is one of our newest tables. We've run the first meetup so far, and it went the way you'd hope: a real conversation about AI safety, a couple of people who left interested enough to start looking into AI research, and a local who offered to host the next one. The next gathering goes up on Luma.

Who?

Smart, curious people who want to talk about AI safety, rationality, and how the world works, including anyone who's just getting interested. There's no application and no vetting. If that sounds like you, come sit down and introduce yourself.

Why?

The goal is a standing weekly table you can count on, because a fixed slot is what turns a one-off meetup into a real social circle. Warsaw is at the start of that. The first night already found its next host, which is exactly how this is meant to grow.

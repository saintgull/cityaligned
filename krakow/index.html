What?

A weekly, low-key gathering for curious and technical people in Kraków. Researchers, builders, and anyone drawn to ideas about AI, rationality, and how the world works. No agenda, no presentations. Just a table, a fixed time, and good conversation.

Who?

Kraków has a strong and growing community of people who think seriously about AI safety, effective altruism, and rationality. This gathering is for them, and for anyone they think would make the table more interesting. If you're the kind of person who reads LessWrong, follows alignment research, or just wants a weekly room of people with your intellectual sensibilities, this is for you.

Why?

Monthly meetups feel like networking. Weekly gatherings build a social circle. Kraków's AI safety and rationalist communities are active but scattered across Meetup groups, university labs, and online forums. A standing weekly slot gives everyone a home base.

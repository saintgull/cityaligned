Kraków

A new table

What?

A low-key weekly gathering for curious and technical people in Kraków, the kind who are drawn to AI safety, rationalism, and how the world works. No agenda and no presentations, just a table at a fixed time and some good conversation. We've run the first meetup, about a dozen people, and the next one goes up on Luma.

Who?

Kraków has a strong and growing community of people who think seriously about AI safety, effective altruism, and rationalism. This is for them, and for anyone they think would make the table more interesting. If you read LessWrong, follow alignment research, or are just getting curious about any of it, this is for you.

Why?

Monthly meetups feel like networking. Weekly gatherings build a social circle. Kraków's AI safety and rationalist communities are active but scattered across Meetup groups, university labs, and online forums. A standing slot gives everyone a home base. The first meetup is done; the work now is making it a habit.

Get involved

If you want to help shape the Kraków table, or host, reach out. Otherwise just watch Luma for the next one and come sit down.
